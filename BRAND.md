# Borgo Guglielmo — Brand Guidelines

## Color Palette

| Name       | Hex       | Usage                          |
|------------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| Cream      | `#e8e4dc` | Primary text on dark, accents  |
| Sand       | `#d4cfc4` | Secondary text, borders        |
| Olive      | `#4a4a40` | Backgrounds, cards             |
| Charcoal   | `#1a1a1a` | Primary dark background        |
| Forest     | `#3d3d35` | Secondary dark, overlays       |
| White      | `#ffffff` | Highlights, contrast text      |

## Typography

**Primary Font:** Mona Sans

| Style      | Weight | Size              | Usage                |
|------------|--------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Display    | 600    | 1–1.25rem         | Headlines, nav       |
| Body       | 400    | 0.875–1rem        | Paragraphs           |
| Caption    | 500    | 0.75rem           | Labels, metadata     |

**Formatting:**
- Headlines: uppercase, 0.05em letter-spacing
- Body: sentence case, normal tracking

## Logo

Text-based wordmark "BORGO GUGLIELMO" in Mona Sans 600.
Minimum clear space: 1x cap height on all sides.

## Photography

- Architectural, natural landscapes
- Muted, earthy tones
- High contrast with soft shadows
- Overlay: rgba(0,0,0,0.4) for text legibility

## Voice & Tone

- Refined, understated
- Warm but professional
- Italian heritage, contemporary execution
